/* ### INTRO ###
For this part of the project, our team aimed to calculate 12 different types of proposals based on clients' requirements and budget/wedding size
constraints. We used the best alternatives for each department based on:

1- Price Analysis.
2- Qualitative Research on Vendors' Quality and Deliverables
3- Proximity to the 'Bohemian Bloom' Theme.
4- Priorities from our clients (e.g. Flowers are high priority while Music is low priority).

For the values we defined for each vendors, we aim to serve a wedding from 3 different sizes based on our assumptions and research:
1- SMALL (50 people)
2- MEDIUM (100 people)
3- LARGE (200 people)

Some of these items quantity will not change based on the size (e.g. wedding dress). However, wedding size will have a direct impact on other items quantity (e.g.
flowers, invitations, catering services, etc.). We added a few extra quantities (~~10% of wedding size) to support extraordinary events such as:
1- People who showed up that were not in the original list of guests.
2- Broken glassware/dineware.
3- Broken tables.

We would like to offer 12 different options that can accomodate our clients' best day of their lives: THE BOHEMIAN BLOOM!

*/
